# Family Memory AI - Ideas

## Purpose

This document tracks ideas that are discussed during the project.

An idea is not part of the roadmap until explicitly approved by the Product Owner.

## Idea Statuses

- Under Evaluation: proposed but not approved yet
- Approved: approved by the Product Owner but not implemented yet
- Planned: approved and assigned to a future Sprint
- Implemented: already built into the application
- Rejected: discussed and explicitly rejected

## Rules

- Ideas must not be added to the roadmap automatically.
- Every idea requires explicit approval from the Product Owner.
- Approval must use the format: APPROVA IDEA X.
- Rejection must use the format: RIFIUTA IDEA X.
- Approved ideas should be documented before implementation.
- Implemented ideas should be moved to Implemented.
