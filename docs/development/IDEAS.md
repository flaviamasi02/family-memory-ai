# Family Memory AI - Ideas

## Purpose

This document tracks ideas discussed during the development of Family Memory AI.

An idea is not part of the roadmap until it is explicitly approved by the Product Owner.

## Rules

- Ideas must be explicitly approved or rejected.
- Approval format: APPROVA IDEA X.
- Rejection format: RIFIUTA IDEA X.
- Approved ideas may be added to the roadmap only after Product Owner confirmation.
- Ideas discussed in chat are not automatically part of the project.
- This file must be kept updated when ideas are approved, rejected, planned or implemented.

## Status Definitions

- Under Evaluation: proposed but not approved yet.
- Approved: approved by the Product Owner but not yet planned.
- Planned: approved and assigned to a future Sprint.
- Implemented: already implemented.
- Rejected: explicitly rejected.
